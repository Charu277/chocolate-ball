CHOCO BALLS(for 4 person)

ingredients:

  • 4 packets chocolate biscuits
  • sugar powder(2-3 tablespoon)
  • melted chocolate(any)
  • grated almonds
  • liquid butter
  • milk(1cup)

    • recipe:

  • grind biscuits finely
  • add sugar powder in grinded chocolate biscuits
  • add milk to make a soft dough
  • apply some liquid butter your hand
  • apply some liquid butter on plate
  • make balls from that chocolate dough
  • refrigerate them
  • garnish with melted chocolate and grated almonds
    • ENJOY YOUR CHOCOBALLS
